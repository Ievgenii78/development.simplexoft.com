Why choose a payday loan?

A payday loan for £700 can help you keep everything in the black when things get tight. Many applicants opt for payday loans when suddenly faced with an unexpected bill. The term is flexible enough to ensure you can afford the repayments, but not so long that you’re saddled with debt for many years.

Can I apply with bad credit?

If your credit score is low, then Payday UK can still help. We specialise in providing loans to people who’ve had problems in the past. We don’t believe in punishing you for past mistakes. Your current situation is more important to us. Our soft credit check won’t affect your credit rating when you apply.

How long will it take to get my loan?

Payday UK provides quick access to a panel of industry-leading direct lenders that are regulated by the FCA. We find the lowest rate on offer for you. If you accept the loan offer, the money will be in your bank within around 10 minutes*.
