More questions? We've got you covered

Are there any guaranteed payday loans?

Unfortunately, there are no guaranteed payday loans. We also get asked if we can provide guaranteed payday loans with no credit check or for people with bad credit. We can’t guarantee you’ll be accepted. However, you can increase the likelihood you’ll be accepted by applying through Payday UK. We only show you a loan offer that you meet the criteria for, so your chance of being approved is far higher than merely applying directly through a website without any checks first.

Is it easy to get approved for a payday loan?

Payday UK has built a wide-ranging panel of lenders to maximise the likelihood of being approved. Some of the lenders on our panel specialise in bad credit payday loans, short term loans, or loans for more significant amounts. With an acceptance rating of 95%, there are very few people we can’t help find a suitable loan, regardless of their situation.

Can I get a payday loan without being credit checked?

You can apply for a loan through Payday UK without a full credit check being performed. Lenders carry out a soft credit check, which leaves no trace on your credit report and won’t affect any future lending decisions. A full credit check is only performed after you have accepted a loan offer. This is because the FCA (The Financial Conduct Authority) regulations make it compulsory for all reputable payday loan lenders before they can release any funds.

Which lender is most likely to approve me?

The answer to this question is different for every applicant. We all have a unique set of circumstances. The best lender for you will be the one that offers the lowest interest rate for your situation. However, this can change from month to month. This is one of the reasons we use such a large pool of lenders, to maximise the chance of approval for everyone that’s trying to access some extra cash.

I have been declined elsewhere. Can you still help me?

Yes. We specialise in helping people with a poor credit rating. If you’ve been denied elsewhere, we may be able to offer you a solution through one of our lender partners. If you complete our application process, we will give you an instant decision on your eligibility, and it won’t harm your credit rating.
