2. Overview

2.1 On choosewisely.co.uk, You can access a number of Tools aimed at helping You understand and identify suitable credit and banking Products. Many of these Tools feature forms that You can use to register, to tell Us about yourself, and to tell Us about the Products that You’re interested in. These forms collect contact details, information about Your personal circumstances, and/or information about Your financial background.

2.2 We collect this information to enable You to access and use the Tools featured on the Website and without it, some Tools and other features may not work at all. We also collect information to understand and give You feedback on Your financial situation, to tailor the results displayed on comparison tables, to make the site more relevant for You, to help speed-up form-filling, to ensure that You only see Products that match Your requirements, and to communicate with You about the services You’ve accessed.

2.3 We collect information about how You use the site - information such as which pages You view, how long You spend reading different pages and which parts of the site You click on. The Choose Wisely team uses this kind of information to understand how good a job choosewisely.co.uk is doing, to identify ways that We can make the Website better and to understand the impact of any changes We make.

2.4 When You visit choosewisely.co.uk, the Website will attempt to leave tiny text files on Your device called “Cookies”. We use Cookies for a number of reasons. Some cookies identify who You are so that We can remember Your settings, some ensure that You have a consistent, personalised experience each time You visit, and others enable Us to carry out research, to analyse market trends and to monitor how successful the Website is.

2.5 Some features on choosewisely.co.uk are aimed at helping You gain an understanding of how eligible You are for certain Products or if certain Providers will accept You if You are to make an application. In these instances, the Website may share Your personal data with Providers so that they can make an accurate assessment, identify You and respond accordingly.

2.6 We may share information about You with other members of Our group of companies, with service Providers who enable Us to host and monitor Our Website, and with regulatory or governmental bodies.
