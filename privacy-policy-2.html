PRIVACY POLICY

 

Loan Machine Limited (we or us) are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy in the delivery of our Services (which are outlined in section 1 below).

By visiting any of our Sites or by providing us with information via the I-Frame Form you are accepting and consenting to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1 Services


1.1 As an introducer and credit broker we will help you search for a loan and provide a matching service as follows (the Services). To help you with your application and search for a loan, we pass your data to a group of lenders and credit brokers on our lending panel (Lending Partners). The lenders on our panel may be willing to enter into a consumer credit agreement with you, subject to their assessment of your creditworthiness and the affordability of the loan among other compliance checks. On submitting your application, we also share your information with other trusted regulated credit brokers who will simultaneously endeavour to match your application with their lending partners. If we find a match, your application is redirected to the lender's website. 


1.2 We have commercial links with our trusted Lending Partners and will generally receive a payment from them (not you) in the form of a commission if we are successful in matching you or if they actually fund a loan. Our panel currently consists of a large panel of lenders and brokers and our matching services only relate to that group, therefore, please note that the Services do not constitute an entire market search for loan products. While our providers have been chosen to provide a variety of products, there are other products on the market that are not available from our panel and, one or more of them may be more appropriate or suitable for you, so you may wish to shop around.

5 Credit Reference and Fraud Prevention Agencies

5.1.1 their own;

6.3 We use the information to compile reports and to help us improve this Site and our other Sites. The information that the cookies enable us to collect, including the number of visitors to this Site, where visitors have come to this Site from and the pages they have visited. The information that we obtain using Google Analytics is anonymous - it does not identify you personally. 


6.4 If you make a loan application on our Site we also need to use a cookie to help with the completion of the online application form. The cookie is deleted when the user closes their browser. 


6.5 You  can decline to give your consent to this Site (and our other Sites) to use cookies but if you do so this may mean that you cannot use some of the features of this Site or our other Sites. 


6.6 More information about the use of cookies can be found at www.allaboutcookies.org and there is information about Google Analytics at:
http://www.google.com/intl/en/analytics/privacyoverview.html.

If you decline to accept our use of cookies or set your web browser to decline their use, you will have only limited functionality in the use of our Sites.
